About Theshu

Theshu (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black female owned private company located in the Tsantsabane area of Postmasburg, Northern Cape. Founded and managed by Mrs. Boitumelo Gladness Shushu, our company brings years of mining industry experience to the service sector.

Our mission is to provide exceptional event management, catering, cleaning, and supply services while empowering unemployed youth within our community. We take pride in setting an example for young females who aspire to become entrepreneurs.

At Theshu, we are committed to achieving Zero Harm to our employees, service providers, and the communities we serve. Our comprehensive safety programme and BBBEE compliance ensure we operate with the highest standards of excellence and integrity.

Mrs. Boitumelo Gladness Shushu

General Director

With years of experience in the mining industry, Mrs. Shushu leads Theshu (Pty) Ltd with a vision to empower communities and create opportunities for young entrepreneurs. Her leadership focuses on excellence, compliance, and social responsibility.
