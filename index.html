Our Story

Built on Purpose,
Driven by Community

Theshu (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black female owned enterprise founded in December 2020, located in the Tsantsabane area of Postmasburg, Northern Cape. We bring mining industry precision to the world of events and services.

Youth Empowerment

Actively employing and mentoring unemployed youth within our community.
