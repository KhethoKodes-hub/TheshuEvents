Our Story

From Vision to
Reality

Theshu (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black female owned private company located in the Tsantsabane area of Postmasburg, Northern Cape. Founded and managed by Mrs. Boitumelo Gladness Shushu, our company brings years of mining industry experience to the service sector.

Established in December 2020, Theshu rapidly grew into a trusted provider of event management, catering, cleaning, and supply services. Our journey began with a vision to create a company that not only delivers exceptional services but also contributes to community development and youth empowerment.

Today, we take pride in our reputation for reliability, quality, and professionalism — earning the trust of clients across private events and government gatherings alike.
