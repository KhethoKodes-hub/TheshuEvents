About Theshu (Pty) Ltd

Learn about our story, values, and commitment to excellence

Our Story

Theshu (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black female owned private company located in the Tsantsabane area of Postmasburg, Northern Cape. Founded and managed by Mrs. Boitumelo Gladness Shushu, our company brings years of mining industry experience to the service sector.

Established in December 2020, Theshu has rapidly grown to become a trusted provider of event management, catering, cleaning, and supply services in the region. Our journey began with a vision to create a company that not only delivers exceptional services but also contributes to community development and youth empowerment.

Today, we take pride in our reputation for reliability, quality, and professionalism. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of clients across various sectors, from private events to government gatherings.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide exceptional event management, catering, cleaning, and supply services while empowering unemployed youth within our community through skills development and employment opportunities.

Our Vision

To be the leading service provider in the Northern Cape region, recognized for excellence, innovation, and our commitment to social responsibility and community development.
