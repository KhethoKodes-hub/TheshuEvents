Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Postmasburg and surrounding communities in the Northern Cape, but can accommodate requests throughout the broader region.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend at least 2–3 weeks for events, and 1 week for cleaning and supply services to guarantee availability and proper planning.

Do you provide quotes?

Yes — we provide free, no-obligation quotes for all services. Send us your requirements and we'll prepare a detailed, transparent estimate.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, EFT, and major credit cards. Flexible payment terms for corporate and government clients can also be arranged.

Do you work with government clients?

Absolutely. We are fully CIPC, SARS, and BEE compliant with an active CSD registration (MAAA1041196), making us eligible for government contracts.

Can you handle multiple services at once?

Yes — we offer complete event packages combining catering, decoration, tent rental, and cleaning, giving you a single, hassle-free point of contact.
