Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Postmasburg area and surrounding communities in the Northern Cape, but we can accommodate requests throughout the region.

How far in advance should I book services?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for events, and 1 week in advance for cleaning and supply services to ensure availability.

Do you provide quotes?

Yes, we provide free, no-obligation quotes for all our services. Contact us with your requirements, and we'll prepare a detailed estimate.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, EFT, and major credit cards. Payment terms for corporate clients can be arranged.
